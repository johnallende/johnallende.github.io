Digital Design

In the 7th grade I was introduced to digital design and the concept of bringing my ideas to life with a screen. Drawing out my thoughts, going through sketches, pondering ideas and just making my visions a reality to show and share with everyone around me. Creating digital art has been my passion for over 5 years now, it's has been a way to express myself on a personal and artistic level .

Youtuber | Film Maker

As a hobbie I have always had an intrest in film making and the idea of becoming a youtuber. the community attracts me and makes me feel at home in way, being surrounded by so many creative people with diffrent perspectives and outlooks on the world that might contrast mine but still interest me none the less. Travel | Exploration

A personal goal I have is to venture out of my comfort zone and explore the world. I want to experience new sights and sounds to become educated with the wonders of the world and possibly gain inspiration for future art pieces or digital designs. I can't help but to think that traveling the world will expand and modify my perspective of diffrent art styles, improving and giving variation to my potential projects.
